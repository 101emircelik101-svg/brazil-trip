The Amazon

The lungs of
our planet

Covering over 5.5 million square kilometres, the Amazon rainforest is home to 10% of all species on Earth. Its canopy stretches so vast it creates its own weather, feeds its own rivers, and whispers in frequencies older than language itself.

To travel here is not to visit a place — it is to enter a living, breathing entity that will change you in ways you will only understand years later.

5.5M km² of forest
40,000+ plant species
3,000+ fish species

Destinations

Six unmissable
wonders

01

Amazônia

Amazonas State

The world's greatest rainforest. Navigate its rivers by wooden boat as pink dolphins surface alongside.

02

Cataratas do Iguaçu

Paraná State

275 cascades wider than Niagara. Stand on the walkway as the mist rises around you like a living cloud.

03

Pantanal

Mato Grosso

Earth's largest tropical wetland. The best place on the planet to see jaguars in the wild.

04

Chapada Diamantina

Bahia State

Tabletop mountains, hidden grottos, and waterfalls that drop into crystal blue swimming holes.

05

Lençóis Maranhenses

Maranhão State

Vast white sand dunes dotted with turquoise lagoons — a dreamscape unlike anywhere on Earth.

06

Rio de Janeiro

Rio State

Cidade Maravilhosa — the marvellous city. Samba, Sugarloaf, and sunsets over the Guanabara Bay.

Curated Experiences

Moments that
define a lifetime

01

Canopy Walk at Dawn

Rise before the jungle does. Walk the suspension bridges high above the forest floor as the dawn chorus reaches its crescendo and mist rises through the canopy below your feet.

Amazon
02

Pink Dolphin Encounter

The boto — the Amazon river dolphin — is unlike anything else on Earth. Glide silently by canoe as these ethereal creatures surface in the black waters of the Rio Negro.

Manaus
03

Jaguar Tracking, Pantanal

With expert trackers, navigate the flooded plains by boat searching for the spotted ghost. The Pantanal offers the highest jaguar density on the planet — your odds have never been better.

Wildlife
04

Wild Swimming, Chapada Diamantina

Descend into caverns where underground rivers have carved chambers of impossible blue. These secret grottos feel like the world's most private swimming pools.

Adventure
05

Sunrise Capoeira on Copacabana

Join a roda as the sun rises over the Atlantic. Watch masters of this Afro-Brazilian martial art-dance weave between each other with impossible grace. Then eat fresh coconut on the sand.
